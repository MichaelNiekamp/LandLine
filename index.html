The Property Management Lifecycle

Track a Property

Add a property to your rolodex to begin keeping a digital timeline of that property. Upload files, connect with tenants and contractors, and manage your property with everything saved to the rolodex.

Report a Problem

To initiate a repair, document issues in a property. Tenants and homeowners submit problem reports to document the issue in the rolodex and provide context to future contractors selected for services.

Request a Servicer

Find the right contractor to solve your reported problem. LandLine automatically provides selected contractors with necessary context and adds all interactions with contractors to the rolodex to eliminate redundandcy and maintain accurate history.

Review a Service

Provide feedback for completed services to encourage quality service. Landlords and tenants can review a contractor's rendered service to influence a servicer's rating.
